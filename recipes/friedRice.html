Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a chinese dish of cooked rice, vegetables, proteins, and other aromatics. The ingridents are pan fried together is a wok because of the intense heat and the circular shape. This recipe is simple to make when you leftover ingridents, so many people make it for dinner.

Ingridents

  • 2/3 cup chopped baby carrots
  • 1/2 cup of frozen peas
  • vegetable oil
  • 2 spoons garlic
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 cups of cold Rice
  • vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil

Steps

  1. Place carrots and peas into a small bowl, and bring them to a low boil for 3-5 minutes
  2. Next, put the heat to high, and put the wok. Pour in carrots, peas and garlic. Then cook the egg. Mix well.
  3. Add in the rice and mix. Add the soy sauce and drizzle in the sesame oil. Keep tossing the rice
  4. Serve on a plate, and enjoy!
